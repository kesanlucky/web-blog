Imperia 2022

A state level Inter-collegiate meet IMPERIA 2k22 organized by the Department of Computer Science, Information Technology and Computer Applications, Mannar Thirumalai Naicker College on 29.04.2022.

The event started with the prayer song and all the dignitarities on the diaz lighted the lamp. Dr.G.Devika, Head welcomed the gathering. Mr.M.Vijayaragavan, Secretary felicitated the gathering. Dr.B.Manoharan, Principal gave the presidential address and Mr.C.Prabhu, Director incharge spoke about the programme. Mrs. R.Vasuki introduced the chief guest. Chief guest Mr.Vimalraj Natarajan, Support Team lead ,Wallmart Labs, Chennai graced the event. He highlighted the skill sets required by the industry. He encouraged the students to develop their personality in all endeavours. Dr.B.Manoharan, Principal declared the ICM Expo. Mr.M. Vijayaragavan, Secretary released the souvenir - LYCEUM. Mrs.R.Vanitha proposed the vote of thanks.

Around 400 students from various colleges participated in the meet. Events such as 'Paper presentation', 'Quiz', 'Web Sketching', 'Face painting', 'Short film' and 'As you like it' were conducted to the participants. Nearly 17 stalls were permitted to utilize the college premises on that day.

Mr.S.R.Kalaivanan, II MSc(CS) proposed the valedictory address. Ms.M. Hemalatha, I MSc(CS) introduced the chief guest, Alumi Mrs.L.Madhavi, Programmer, HCL Madurai. The alumni addressed the students about the recent opportunities of young generation.

Dr.A.Ramasubbiah, IQAC and NAAC Coordinator, Secretary, Director i/c and Alumni L.Madhavi distributed the prizes. Dr.G.Devika Head , Mrs.R.Vanitha, Mrs.R.Vasuki and Dr.S.Shaik Parveen coordinated the program. VHNSN College, Virudhunagar emerged Overall winner, Yadava College,Madurai finished overall runner-up and American College,Madurai won the special prize.
