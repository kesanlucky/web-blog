About our department

The department of Computer Science was started in the year 1998, under the self-financed section and is affiliated to Madurai Kamaraj University. The department offers Bachelor of Computer Science B.Sc(CS) and Master of Computer Science M.Sc(CS). The undergraduate Computer Science at Mannar Thirumalai Naicker College acknowledges the relevance of computing and information science to every academic discipline, and emphasizes exposure to drive innovation in the future. They will not only develop a diverse set of skills to prepare for graduate school and for employment, but will also be encouraged to launch their own startups or venture into new types of careers using their interdisciplinary training. It aims at developing technically progressive, globally competent and dynamic CS professionals. It grooms the students by offering a blend of technical and soft skills to meet corporate expectations. The department has well qualified, experienced and dedicated faculty members and it possesses well equipped laboratory.
