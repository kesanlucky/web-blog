ONE DAY INDUSTRIAL VISIT

A batch of final year UG and pg students of Department of Computer Science, Computer Applications and Information technology along with faculty visited Kodaikanal Solar Observatory in Kodaikanal.

This visit was mainly focused on to understand the procedures involved the technology and the equipment's used in astrophysics. Technical staffs are explained about the facilities available inside the centre, their methods used in the system. The material displayed here is informative and one can revise & revisit science concepts especially regarding the solar system. Industrial visit was successfully coordinated by Mrs. R.Vasuki and Dr.S.Veerapandi from the Department of Computer Science. Under the guidance of Dr.G.Devika ,Head of the Department and the staff members Mrs.R.Vanitha, Dr.S.Shaik parveen, Dr.T.Sujithra and Mr.R.Ramesh Kumar were also engaged.
