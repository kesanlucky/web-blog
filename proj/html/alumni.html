Alumni Interaction Programme

An Alumni Interaction Programme was conducted on 22nd April 2022 by Computer Science Department at Mannar Thirumalai Naicker College. Dr. G. Devika Head, Department of Science welcomed the gathering. Mrs.S. Amutha and Mrs. T.Sujithra organized this Alumni Interaction Programme.

Our Department Alumni 2018-2021 Batch, B.Sc(CS), Mr. A.Argajan, Project Trainee, Chennai participated and delivered the keynote address on the topic “Guideline for Industrial Entry” This programme organized to all 3rd year B.Sc(CS), B.Sc(IT), B.C.A. Alumni interacted with the students and shared his college experience and working experience. Students were motivated through this programme. Students come to know many opportunities to help start their professional career with this meet. During this time, it's important to focus on their career aspirations, plan for the future. This programme also help them to become more confident in their skills and ensure they have chosen the right field as well as guide them toward making helpful career decisions.
